# Code of Conduct

SimOffice welcomes contributors who want to build useful, understandable, and human-centered AI tooling.

Be respectful. Be specific. Critique ideas and code, not people. Assume good intent, but value clarity over ambiguity.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, hateful conduct, doxxing, sexualized comments, and repeated disruption of project work.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who harm the project community.
