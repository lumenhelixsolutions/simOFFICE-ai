# Commercialization Notes

SimOffice is open source, but its product direction is commercial-grade.

## Possible commercial layers

- Hosted SimOffice Cloud.
- Team workspaces and auth.
- Managed connector gateway.
- Enterprise audit logs.
- Workflow template marketplace.
- Premium station packs.
- Consulting and custom agent operations floors.

## Open-core boundary suggestion

Keep the core 3D UI, local backend bridge, furniture registry, and local agent management open source.

Commercial layers can focus on hosted reliability, compliance, team management, enterprise connectors, analytics, and managed deployments.

## Buyer language

SimOffice helps teams make AI agents manageable. It turns invisible agent prompts into visible operators with roles, tools, approval policies, and workstations.

## Use cases

- Marketing operations center.
- Research and analysis desk.
- Founder command room.
- Internal AI operations dashboard.
- Client delivery studio.
- Local-first AI control plane.
